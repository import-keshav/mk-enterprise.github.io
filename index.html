About MK Enterprise

Leading Industrial Handloom Manufacturing Unit in North India

Established in 2000, MK Enterprise is a large-scale handloom manufacturing unit in Panipat, Haryana serving both domestic and export markets. Our 100+ advanced machines enable wholesale production for B2B clients, distributors, and international buyers seeking export-quality handloom products.

Our Heritage

Located in the textile hub of Panipat, we carry forward the rich legacy of Indian handloom traditions. With over 25 years in the industry, our skilled artisans bring decades of experience in creating premium quality bathmats, rugs, and decorative mats that blend functionality with aesthetic appeal.

Quality & Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices, supporting local communities, and maintaining the highest quality standards. Every product undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure durability and excellence.
